About Me

Hi, I’m Marie Ament — a staff systems test engineer with a background in cybersecurity and a passion for smart, secure tech. I specialize in embedded systems, automation, and testing electric meter integrations. With hands-on experience in Python, Wireshark, and Selenium, I love solving complex problems and working across teams to deliver reliable solutions. Outside of tech, I enjoy hiking with my fiancé and dogs, snowboarding, and playing recreational sports.
